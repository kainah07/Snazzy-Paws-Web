Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Statement explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information we collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal details such as your name, email, phone number, and pet information when booking an appointment.

  • Usage data, including how you interact with our website (e.g., page visits, browsing behavior) through cookies and analytics tools.

Why we collect your information

We collect your information to:

  • Process and manage grooming appointments.

  • Improve our services and customer experience.

  • Provide you with updates, reminders, or promotional offers (with your consent).

How We Use and Share Your Information

  • Your information is used only for the purposes stated above.

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when necessary to provide our services (e.g., booking confirmations).

What Happens If You Don't Provide Information?

Providing personal information is optional, but without it, we may be unable to:

  • Process your booking

  • Contact you regarding your appointment

  • Provide personalized services

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of any personal information we hold about you and to ask for corrections if you believe it is inaccurate. To make a request, please contact us at admin@snazzypawsgrooming.nz.
